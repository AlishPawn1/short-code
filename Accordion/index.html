Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my website goes down or is hacked?

All our Website Maintenance plans include Emergency downtime support, what this entails is us restoring the website to a previous backup. The idea behind this is to get your website back up and running as quickly as possible

Do my monthly hours & tasks roll over?

Yes, we operate on a fair-use policy so that we can deliver a high level of service to all our clients. It will be rolled over next month only.

What if I need something that isn’t covered by my Website Maintenance plan?

We can usually help with this! We’ll be able to quote you, and get your approval for the quote prior to going ahead with any additional work required.
